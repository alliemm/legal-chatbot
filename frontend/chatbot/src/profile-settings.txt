========================================================
USER PROFILE SETTINGS — CHATBOT LEGAL ASSISTANT
========================================================

--------------------------------------------------------
1. FOCUS AREAS  (clause types the chatbot highlights)
--------------------------------------------------------
Options (multi-select, any combination allowed):

  - Privacy & data collection clauses
      Highlight how personal data is collected, stored, shared,
      or sold. Flag broad consent language and opt-out limitations.

  - Fee & payment clauses
      Surface all costs, automatic renewals, late-payment penalties,
      price-change rights, and hidden charges.

  - Termination & cancellation conditions
      Emphasize how either party may end the agreement, required
      notice periods, early-exit fees, and what happens to data
      after cancellation.

  - Liability & indemnification clauses
      Call out limitations of liability, indemnity obligations,
      and waivers of legal rights.

  - Intellectual property & ownership clauses
      Flag who owns content the user creates, license grants to
      the platform, and work-for-hire provisions.

  - Dispute resolution & arbitration clauses
      Highlight mandatory arbitration, class-action waivers, and
      choice-of-law / jurisdiction provisions.

  - Auto-renewal & subscription terms
      Surface automatic rollover language, free-trial-to-paid
      conversions, and cancellation windows.

  - Non-compete & non-solicitation clauses
      Flag restrictions on future employment, client poaching
      prohibitions, and geographic/time limits.

--------------------------------------------------------
2. EXPLANATION STYLE
--------------------------------------------------------
Options (single-select):

  - Plain language   — simple, jargon-free summaries
  - Balanced         — mix of plain language with key legal terms defined
  - Technical        — precise legal terminology preserved

--------------------------------------------------------
3. RESPONSE DETAIL LEVEL
--------------------------------------------------------
Options (single-select):

  - Brief    — 2–3 sentence summary per clause
  - Standard — paragraph-length explanation with key points called out
  - Detailed — full breakdown including implications, examples, and risk rating

--------------------------------------------------------
4. RISK SENSITIVITY
--------------------------------------------------------
Options (single-select):

  - Low    — flag only severe / clearly one-sided clauses
  - Medium — flag moderately risky or unusual clauses (default)
  - High   — flag anything that deviates from standard practice

--------------------------------------------------------
5. LANGUAGE PREFERENCE
--------------------------------------------------------
Options (single-select):

  - English
  - 中文 (Chinese)
  - Español
  - Français
  - Deutsch
  - Other (free text)

--------------------------------------------------------
6. DOCUMENT CONTEXT
--------------------------------------------------------
Stored from onboarding survey; editable at any time:

  - User type           : Individual | Small business owner | Nonprofit | Other
  - Legal experience    : No prior lawyer | Past lawyer | Currently has lawyer
  - Document frequency  : Rarely | A few times a year | Monthly | Weekly
  - Primary goal        : Understand what I'm signing
                        | Identify risks before I sign
                        | Know when I need a real lawyer
                        | Build or negotiate a stronger document
                        | Settle a dispute

--------------------------------------------------------
7. CHATBOT BEHAVIOUR FLAGS
--------------------------------------------------------
Toggle on/off:

  - Always show a risk rating (Low / Medium / High) for each clause
  - Recommend consulting a lawyer when risk is High
  - Show relevant deadlines and key dates at the top of every summary
  - Include a one-line plain-English TL;DR at the start of each response
  - Proactively ask clarifying questions if the document context is unclear

--------------------------------------------------------
8. NOTIFICATION & HISTORY PREFERENCES
--------------------------------------------------------
  - Save document analysis history   : Yes | No
  - Email digest of past analyses    : Never | Weekly | Monthly
  - Allow anonymous usage analytics  : Yes | No
